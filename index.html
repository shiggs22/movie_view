About Us

Here at Movie View Review, we take the time to watch movies from past to present and give you information about the movies. We love movies and I'm sure, since you are at this site, you love them too! So, every month we will choose a movie to write about and give you feed back on whether or not you should see it. if you should watch this movie or not. As you can see, we give a brief description on the movie, some favorite quotes, and information on the main Actors themselves. Feel free to look around and sign up to our site so you can add your thoughts on the movie, your favorite quotes, and your recommendation.
